bandcamp

Trivalent audio is available in various formats (FLAC, Ogg Vorbis, &c.) for direct download, as well as in 128kb/s MP3 for streaming, from the Trivalent bandcamp page. You can also choose to pay some amount of social points money for it, if you want.

failover

Bandcamp has a form of rate-limiting for free-of-charge downloads, in the form of download tokens. If you pay actual money for the audio, the download should(?) be guaranteed to work, but otherwise it’s possible that the Trivalent bandcamp account runs out of free download tokens. In that case, you can still obtain the audio freely, but you will have to use a different host for downloading instead.

Since all Trivalent creative assets (including audio) are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike license (version 4.0+), you may be able to find redistributions of them around the internet. If not, see the table below for links to audio downloads. Some of these links may be broken (either because they are intentionally ephemeral, or just due to general link rot). If you cannot find a usable download, head over to the contact page and ask about getting a download; that way links can be repaired and/or new ephemeral downloads can be hooked up.
